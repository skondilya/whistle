Whistle

The app allows you to press a single button when you are in an emergency situation and alert a group of up to two friends exactly where you are by sending then email with the link of you google map location.
The Timer section requires you set the time of your journey before you start. In case you don't check in from an outdoor trip or other event by your scheduled return time, the app will automatically send an email to your emergency contacts (it can be your friends or your family memeber).
