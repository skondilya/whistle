Whistle

The app allows you to press a single button when you are in an emergency situation and alert up to two friends exactly where you are by sending then email with the link of your location in google maps.
The "Get Me There" section requires you enter the amount of time your journey will take. You enter the time before you actually start your journey. In case you don't get home from an outdoor trip or other event by your scheduled return time, the app will automatically send an email to your emergency contacts (it can be your friends or a family member).
